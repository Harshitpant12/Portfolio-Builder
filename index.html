FAQ

Q1. Is it really free to start?

A: Yes! Craftolio is completely free to use — you can create and download your portfolio without paying anything.

Q2: Do I need coding skills?

A: Not at all! Craftolio generates everything for you, just fill in your details and choose a template.

Q3: Can I download or share my portfolio?

A: Yes! You can download your portfolio anytime and share it wherever you want.

Q4: Can I edit my portfolio after generating it?

A: Yes! You can return anytime, update your details, and generate a new version within seconds.

Q5: Do you store my personal information?

A: No. Your data is only used to generate your portfolio and is not stored permanently.

Q6: Do the templates work on all devices?

A: Yes. Every Craftolio template is fully responsive and looks great on mobile, tablet, and desktop.

Q7: Will my portfolio work without internet?

A: Yes! Once downloaded, your portfolio is fully offline compatible, it works even without a connection.

Q8: Can I host my portfolio online?

A: Yes. You can upload your HTML file to platforms like GitHub Pages, Netlify, Vercel, or any hosting service.

Q9: Will my images and data stay private?

A: Yes. Everything stays on your device. Craftolio doesn't permanently store or share your content.

Q10: Do I need to install anything?

A: No installation needed. Craftolio runs directly in your browser—simple and fast.
