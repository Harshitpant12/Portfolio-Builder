About Craftolio

Your portfolio, designed beautifully in minutes, no coding required.

What is Craftolio?

Craftolio is a fast, beginner-friendly portfolio builder designed to help students, developers, and creators build clean, professional portfolios in minutes — without coding.

Why we built it

Most people struggle to create a portfolio because:

  • coding takes time,
  • templates online are complicated,
  • file structure can be confusing,
  • and design isn't everyone's strength.

Craftolio solves all of this with a simple idea:
Just enter your details, pick a template, and download your portfolio instantly.

How it works

Craftolio uses pre-designed responsive templates that auto-fill your information live. Nothing gets stored on a server; everything happens on your device. Once you're done, you simply download your portfolio as a complete HTML or ZIP file.

What makes Craftolio different

  • Zero coding required
  • Instant download
  • 100% free to use
  • Fast, simple, and beginner-friendly
  • Designed templates that work everywhere
  • Full control — your data never stays with us

Our Goal

We want to make portfolio creation effortless. Whether you're applying for internships, jobs, hackathons, or freelancing opportunities, Craftolio helps you present your work beautifully — in minutes, not hours.
